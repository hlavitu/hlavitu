hmaare baare mae / About Us

"Hindi Literary Association" is a one of its kind and the most dynamic student's club in VIT university working towards promoting Hindi Language skills amongst the cosmopolitan enivronment of the campus. Started in "2006" by a couple of Vitians, the club has been progressing swiftly based upon the uniqueness and quality of events conducted by it throughout the year.

hmaarI YaarNaa / Our Idea

Hindi Literary Association (HLA) working tirelessly towards the growth, progress and promotion of Hindi Language, the mother tongue of the majority of Indian population. Club promote Hindi Literature and provide a platform to Hindi speaking students to showcase their literary skills. To help other students who are interested in Hindi Literature and History gain an insight into the same. We conduct various events with Hindi being the medium that prove to be an epitome of creativity. There's hardly any area where we haven't left a mark. When it comes to social awareness, we embrace it with full responsibility.

zaanaHaePa / Domains

Literary Domain

The Literary Department works round the year, penning down a variety of poetry and stories to keep readers informed and entertained. It also writes scripts and outlines for a series of events like skits and dramas performed in the club. Also, here in this department, one improves their knowledge and grip on Hindi and Urdu language.

Management Domain

The Management department deals with all the management and coordination related domains. This department works from the very first step of conducting any event. The Department has the responsibility of approaching and bringing sponsors to the club, collaborating with other clubs and increasing the outreach of the club. It teaches the candidates to handle all real-life problems quite efficiently as well.

Design and Art

The Design and Art department of HLA takes care of all the hand-drawn and digital art and craftwork of the various events conducted by the club. We morph the imagination sprouting from our members into reality by sharing various design concepts. It also works on honing one's digital designing skills via various workshops and guidance.

Media and Creativity

Whether big or small, the M&C department in Hindi Literary Association is on the loose to cover even the minutest details and put them under the limelight. Interacting with the audience, making sure the excitement prevails and nurturing anchors within the club is a job partaken by the MNC department. It takes us to various acoustic web series and mind-calming podcasts.
