Kavi Sammelan
Kavi Sammelan is one of our flagship events, where people enjoy the art of poetry. The event is conducted on the occasion of Hindi Diwas (National Hindi Day), where the elite poets join us and share their art.
Jashn - E - Azadi
Jashn - E - Azadi is also one of our flagship events, where we salute the victory and freedom of our nation on the occasion of Independence Day. We celebrate the event by performing different form of arts like poetry, stories, songs & music, etc.
Vaakyudh
Vaakyudh is one of our premiere events of Riviera. Conflicting political views, grievous protests on streets, ruined friendships and trending Instagram stories, arguments on social media over the controversial citizenship law and the National Register of Citizens are gaining the attention of young and old alike. Witness the resolute debate.
Sur Sangam
While growing up, were you running under the risk of getting intoxicated by Bollywood? Are you that "chalta phirta encyclopedia" of all the filmy dialogues which are at the tip of your lips? Check out, HLA's much awaited premium Riviera event SUR-SANGAM, which will quench the filmy "keeda" in you!
Technical Housie
Technical Housie is a collaborative event with IEEE - PCS VIT. The event stands as a solid rock during the graVITas, every year. If you think you're a tech geek then why don't you accept our challenge and meet us here.
Anunaad
Anunaad means resonance, and HLA presents such an event for one to resonate their voice and share the feelings/thoughts with others and take them on the same journey to witness it live.
